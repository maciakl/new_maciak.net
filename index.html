Who is Luke Maciak?

Luke is a professional software developer, and systems administrator. He specializes in full back end development for the LAMP stack, as well as hacking in Python and Ruby. Luke has also experience in front end web design, programming in the .NET environment and Java. He has also worked as a DBA, Win/Lin systems administrator.

Luke is currently working as a Systems Administrator at a small financial firm in New Jersey.

Luke has been teaching technology at Montclair State University since 2005.
